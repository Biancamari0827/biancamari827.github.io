With a wicked stepmother and two jealous stepsisters who keep her enslaved and in rags, Cinderella stands no chance of attending the royal steampunk ball, till her fairy godmother appears and magically transforms her reality into a dream come true. With the fairy godmothers help Cinderella enchants the handsome Prince Charming at the ball, but must face the wrath of her enraged stepmother and sisters when the spell wears off at midnight, but unbeknownst to Cinderella when the clock stroke midnight, she was kidnapped by the evil Dr. ShadowWalker, changing the steampunk fairytale world forever. Now classic characters must travel across the fairytale realms, completing various missions, fending off fearsome monsters, and collecting fairytale treasures in order to rescue Cinderella and save their happily ever storylines.
